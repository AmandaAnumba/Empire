EMPIRE

A community-centric experience built to empower you.

Our Mission

To inspire a world where women of all backgrounds have equal access to the opportunities and resources that will help them excel personally and professionally.

The Three Pillars

Inspire

Inspire a vision. Empire shares stories that inspire young women looking for the right path in life and answers the questions they have yet to ask.

Rise

Rise to the challenge. As young women go about embracing their calling in life, Empire is here to teach and support them.

Reign

Reign. Empire celebrates the achievements of young minority women and invites them to share their stories with the Empire community.

It’s the three pillars that hooked me – Inspire, Rise, Reign. The feeling that everyone gets a seat at the table…I think the abundance and authority that exudes from Empire would benefit many women.

- Alexandria Bobbitt, Student at Northwestern University

An In-Depth Look

  • Empire is a virtual and in-person experience with the purpose of enabling millennial minority women to have the knowledge, resources, and community they need to live their best lives.

    Empire’s web-site functions as the hub of information and communication: using three focus pillars (Inspire, Rise, and Reign), users will be connected to in-depth content curated by prominent and established female leaders, young up-and-comers, and community members working to build their own path.

  • In tandem to the site, users will also be keyed in to national meetups and exclusive events, culminating in a celebratory Summit at the end of the year. The Summit will commemorate the achievements of Empire and its community: the Empire Awards, hack-a-thons, one-on-ones with corporate sponsors, and a concert are a few examples of the elements that will be built into Empire’s annual Summit.

  • Besides being the first site focused on the careerist millennial woman, Empire will also innovate in the digital space by form and function. The timing of the site operates on “cycles”, which are periods of time in which content focused on one overarching theme is constantly available. Conversation will be supported by a built-in comment system, allowing users to engage within any and all parts of the content that is posted. As the site grows, Empire will evolve to learn each user’s unique interests, and cater their version of the site – in addition to the upcoming mobile application – to their unique searches.

Staff Writers

  • Jessica Gaddis

    Jessica is an inquisitive mind who spends the majority of her time trying to pay attention to the lack luster task in front of her instead of the amazingly beautiful world around her because, unfortunately, tasks pay bills and beautiful worlds do not. Most days are spent playing around inside her own mind and finding new ways to channel intangible thoughts into literal actions.

    Jasmine Jones

    Jasmine will be covering all things Fashion for Empire. She is originally from a small town in Michigan, where she grew up not really putting much thought into her wardrobe. Upon her junior year in high school, she began to realize the intrinsic confidence and beauty that comes from embracing your own personal style, and the power that can come from utilizing your body as a canvas to communicate who you are to the world. She began reading fashion magazines like Vogue and W monthly, so that she could see what styles and trends she liked and could tailor to her own personal style.

    She has lived in Paris, visited London, Barcelona, Switzerland, Rome, Florence, Venice, and traveled across the county, Mexico, and the Caribbean. With every experience she's had, she has taken and used as a catalyst for incorporating different cultures into her own personal wardrobe. So, if you’re looking for the perfect dress for a date, the right wardrobe for work, or a flawless outfit for a night out with the girls, you’ve come to the right place!

    Naakie Nartey

    Naakie Nartey is a 23-year-old dynamic young professional from Atlanta, Georgia. She spent her undergraduate collegiate years at The Georgia Institute of Technology. In May of 2014, she graduated with highest honors, receiving a BSBA with a concentration in Supply Chain and Logistics. Shortly after graduating, she began her professional post-graduate career with one of the world’s top consumer goods companies, where she currently works as a category analyst.

    Although her professional career may be quite traditional, this spunky and outgoing young gal often likes to take the road less traveled. Whether she is jet setting to a foreign country to experience new cultures, or jumping out of an airplane plane fifteen thousand feet in the air, Naakie is always up for an adventure.

    But living life on the edge is not her only modus operandi. She has always been passionate about the financial independence and intelligence of young professional minorities like herself. She believes that while it is important to share her knowledge of how to live a financially savvy life, it is even more impactful to lead by example. She prides herself on proving that you can travel internationally and shop top brands all while still putting fifty percent of your paycheck into a money market account and watching your bank statements grow each month.
